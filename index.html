THE PLATFORM FOR CONNECTED TRANSPORTATION

We are completely redesigning existing transportation systems with the goal of reducing the number of road deaths from 1.3 million every year to zero.

Communication will become the backbone of transportation. In the future, having an integrated communication platform will be as important as having roads and highways nowadays.









PERSONALIZED FLEET ANALYTICS

Our first step to create our platform is to develop a virtual assistant for fleets. We use a combination of real-time communication, external data and sensor fusion to generate personalized recommendations, fine-grained analytics and real-time insights. We strongly believe in maximizing every driver's safety and efficiency.
